Mini Cooper Counts

The objective of the game is to count Mini Coopers

The Rules

  1. The first rule of Mini Cooper Counts: you do not talk about Mini Cooper Counts.
  2. The second rule of Mini Cooper Counts: you DO NOT talk about Mini Cooper Counts!
  3. The third rule of Mini Cooper Counts: if someone yells "Mini Cooper!", it counts.

The Way

  • When you see a Mini Cooper, yell "Mini Cooper"
  • Take a picture of it
  • Share your picture on the WhatsApp group
  • Celebrate as your clan thrashes the others
  • Scores will be reset on the first day of the month
  • If it's your birthday you get 10 points
