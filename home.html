How to play

  • When you see a Mini Cooper, yell "Mini Cooper"
  • Take a picture of it
  • Share your picture on the Mini Cooper Whatsapp group
  • Pester the Mini Cooper admins (Callum and Malachy) if your score doesn't update
  • Celebrate as your clan thrashes the others
  • The clan with the highest scores at the end of the month wil be the winner
  • Scores will be reset on the first day of the month
  • No using pictures off the internet
  • If it's your birthday you get 10 points
